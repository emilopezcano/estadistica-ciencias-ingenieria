\[F = \frac{\frac{ 1}{ n_1}\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n_1} X_i^2}{\frac{ 1}{ n_2}\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n_2} Y_i^2}\sim F_{n_1,n_2}\] * \(E[F] = \frac{n}{n-2}, \; n>2\)

  • \(V[F] = \frac{2n^2(m+n-2)}{m(n-2)^2(n-4)}, \; n>4\)
